# Fund jMonkeyEngine

You can support the engine and its related services through the project collective.

## Open Collective

[Donate via Open Collective](https://opencollective.com/jmonkeyengine)

Open Collective supports:

- PayPal
- credit cards
- bank transfers

The funds are managed by the core developers and used for engine and service maintenance.

## What Support Helps Fund

- hosting and infrastructure
- website and community services
- development and maintenance time
- ecosystem tooling

Backers can also leave a message to the community that may be featured on the homepage.
